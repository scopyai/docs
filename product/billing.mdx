Billing is managed per workspace.

## Billing page

The billing area shows the selected workspace's current plan, credit balance, usage history, and available subscription actions.

Depending on workspace state, you may see actions to:

- Start the one-time **$1 trial**.
- Subscribe to **Premium** or **Ultra**.
- Open the billing portal.
- Change plans.
- Cancel a subscription.
- Contact the founder about **Enterprise**.

Billing changes apply to the selected workspace, not to every workspace connected to your user account.

## Plans

| Plan              | Price        | Usage                                                        |
| ----------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Starter trial** | $1 one-time  | Enough for roughly 3–5 pull request reviews on the workspace |
| **Premium**       | $19.99/month | Monthly review usage allowance included                      |
| **Ultra**         | $99.99/month | Higher monthly review usage allowance included               |
| **Enterprise**    | Custom       | Tailored usage, onboarding, and support                      |

The **$1 trial** is available once per user account. It unlocks review usage for the workspace without starting a subscription. After the trial is used, choose a monthly plan to keep reviewing pull requests.

**Enterprise** is handled manually: you can always email founder at [matt@scopy.dev](mailto:matt@scopy.dev?subject=Enterprise%20plan) with any questions.

## Credits and usage

Scopy tracks billing tier and credit balance on each workspace. Review runs debit credits based on model and vector usage recorded during the run.

- Paid monthly plans include a usage allowance that resets each billing period.
- When a workspace runs out of credits, new reviews are skipped until you add usage through the trial, upgrade, or wait for the next billing reset.
